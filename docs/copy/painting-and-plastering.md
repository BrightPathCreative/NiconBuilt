## Headline
Painting and Plastering Melbourne

A clean finish depends on what's underneath it. We repair the plaster properly first, then paint.

## Page copy
A fresh coat of paint is one of the fastest ways to change how a home feels. But the finish depends entirely on what's underneath it. Cracked plaster, poorly patched walls, sloppy edge work. You notice it within a week, even if you couldn't name it at the time.

We handle painting and plastering together. That means the walls are right before anyone touches a brush. Plaster repairs, cornice repairs, heritage plasterwork, full replasters where needed. Then a clean, consistent paint finish throughout.

In Melbourne's older homes this matters more than most. Federation ceilings, ornate cornices, high plaster walls. These take more care and a different approach. We've been working inside Melbourne's inner south period housing stock for three decades. We know how to treat it properly.

## What's included

- Interior painting, walls, ceilings and trim
- Exterior painting and repainting
- Plaster repairs and patching
- Cornice repairs and replacement
- Heritage and set plaster work
- Full replasters

## FAQs

**Do you repair plaster before painting?** Always. Painting over cracked or damaged plaster doesn't fix the underlying problem. We repair the plaster properly first, let it cure, then paint. The result holds up significantly better.

**Can you match existing paintwork or cornices in a period home?** In most cases, yes. For period homes with heritage colours or specific cornice profiles, we work with what's there and match as closely as possible.

**Do you do single rooms or just full houses?** Both. A single room repaint is fine. A full interior and exterior is fine too. We take on jobs of all sizes and handle every one to the same standard.

**How long does interior painting typically take?** A single room is typically one to two days. A full interior repaint depends on the size of the home and the condition of the walls. Nicon Built provides a realistic timeline and clear quote before any work starts.
