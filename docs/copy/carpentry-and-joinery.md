Carpentry and Joinery Melbourne

## Page copy
Good carpentry is the difference between a home that feels right and one that doesn't quite. It's the door that actually closes properly. The deck that looks like it's always been there. The shelving that fits the space like it was built for it, because it was.

We handle residential carpentry across Melbourne's inner south. Decking and pergolas, doors and frames, custom cabinetry and shelving, skirting boards and architraves. The kind of work that gets done once and doesn't need revisiting.

In Melbourne's period homes, carpentry requires more care. Victorian and Edwardian joinery has its own profiles, proportions, and character. We source period-appropriate materials and match existing details so the new work sits naturally alongside the original, not against it.

## What's included

- Decking and pergola construction
- Internal and external door installation and replacement
- Custom cabinetry and shelving
- Skirting boards, architraves and cornices
- Period-accurate joinery for heritage homes
- Structural framing for extensions and alterations
- Outdoor entertaining structure construction

## FAQs

**Do you do custom carpentry for period homes?** Yes. Victorian and Edwardian homes have specific joinery details that need to be matched properly. We source period-appropriate materials and profiles so new work sits naturally alongside the original.

**Can you build decking and outdoor structures?** Yes. Decking, pergolas, and outdoor entertaining structures are a regular part of what we do across Melbourne's inner south. Every job is built to last and finished properly.

**Can carpentry be coordinated as part of a larger renovation?** Yes. Most carpentry work runs alongside other trades as part of a kitchen, bathroom, or extension project. Nick coordinates the sequencing so carpentry happens at the right stage and nothing holds up the rest of the job.

**What areas do you cover?** Melbourne's inner south and bayside suburbs, including Port Melbourne, Albert Park, Brighton, Armadale, Malvern, South Yarra, Caulfield, Elsternwick, Camberwell, Hawthorn, Kew, and surrounding areas.
