## Page copy
Electrical work touches almost everything in a home. Lighting, power points, switchboards, appliances, outdoor areas. When it's done well, you don't think about it. When it's not, you're chasing problems for years.

We handle residential electrical across Melbourne's inner south. New circuits and power points, lighting installations, switchboard upgrades, outdoor electrical for entertaining areas. Work that's done to code, tested, and signed off properly.

Within a renovation, electrical sequencing matters. Rough-in before the walls go up. Fit-off after the painting. We coordinate every stage so the electrician is there at the right time and the job flows without interruption.

## FAQs

**Do you handle electrical as part of a renovation?** Yes. Electrical is a standard part of most kitchen and bathroom renovations and we coordinate it with every other trade on the job.

**Can you add power points or lighting to an existing home?** Yes. Adding power points, upgrading lighting, or running new circuits in an existing home is work we handle regularly across Melbourne's inner south.

**Is all electrical work licensed?** All electrical work is carried out by licensed electricians. Nick coordinates the trade as the registered builder responsible for the overall project.

**Can you upgrade an older switchboard?** Yes. Switchboard upgrades are a common job, particularly in Melbourne's older homes where the existing board isn't suitable for modern electrical loads.
