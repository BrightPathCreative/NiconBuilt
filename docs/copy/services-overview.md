## Service summaries (each links to its own dedicated page)

### Heritage Renovations and Restorations
Heritage restoration is at the heart of what we do. We specialise in Victorian, Edwardian, and Federation homes, combining over 30 years of experience with a deep understanding of traditional building methods and materials.

Working on heritage properties requires more than quality craftsmanship. It demands experience, careful planning, and respect for the original character of the home. From hidden structural challenges to matching existing materials and complying with heritage requirements, we know what to expect and how to manage it.

Our goal is to preserve the integrity of your home while delivering a result that stands the test of time.

### Heritage Home Extensions
Extending a heritage home in Melbourne's inner south is not the same as a standard extension. It requires a careful balance between new and old, ensuring the original building's character is respected while meeting strict heritage overlay requirements.

Materials, detailing, and construction methods all need to be considered so the new work integrates seamlessly with the existing home, rather than competing with it. Every decision matters, from design through to execution.

With decades of experience in heritage construction, we understand what's required to achieve additions that feel natural, compliant, and true to the original architecture.

### Kitchen Renovations
A quality kitchen renovation in an older Melbourne home requires more than cabinetry. It needs a licensed builder to coordinate the full scope of works.

Behind every kitchen are structural adjustments, plumbing, electrical, tiling, plastering, and precise finishing that all need to come together in sequence. We manage every stage of the process, coordinating all required trades so the work is completed efficiently and to a high standard. You brief us once, and we take care of the rest.

### Bathroom Renovations
Getting a bathroom right means getting the sequencing right. Waterproofing before tiling. Plumbing before fixtures. Every trade in the right order, held to the right standard. Nick manages the whole thing on every bathroom job we take on.

### Home Renovations and Extensions
You've done the planning, got the architect's drawings sorted, now you need someone to build it right. That's where we come in. Kitchens, bathrooms, extensions, structural work. One person on your job the whole way through, no handoffs.

### Trades and Maintenance
Coordinating trades is a pain. We do it so you don't have to. Painters, tilers, plumbers, plasterers, bricklayers. Brief us once. We handle every trade from there and make sure the work is done properly.

### New Builds
Custom homes and dual-occupancy builds across the inner suburbs. If your plans and permits are sorted, we can get moving fast. Still in the design phase? We can help you get there too.
