## Intro
Whatever your home needs, we handle it. Everyday trades like plumbing, electrical, tiling, painting, and carpentry. Pest control, cleaning, and roofing. Kitchen and bathroom renovations. Full extensions and new builds. Heritage restoration for Melbourne's period homes.

We've been working across Melbourne's inner south and bayside suburbs for over 30 years. Every job is managed by the same team, held to the same standard, and delivered with clear communication throughout. You brief us once. We handle the rest.

## Emergency Make Safe and Insurance Work
When something goes wrong — storm damage, burst pipes, fire or flood — making the property safe comes first. Nicon Built responds promptly to emergency make-safe calls across Melbourne's inner south. We secure the site, prevent further damage, and document everything insurers need from day one.

For insurance claims, we work directly with homeowners and adjusters. Scope of works, quotes, progress reports, and completion certificates. As a VBA licensed builder with 30 years of experience, we know what insurers expect and how to keep the process moving.

- Emergency make-safe attendance
- Storm, fire, flood and impact damage
- Temporary securing and weatherproofing
- Insurance scope assessment and quoting
- Claim documentation and progress reporting
- Restoration and rebuild coordination

### Kitchen Renovations
A quality kitchen renovation in an older Melbourne home requires more than cabinetry. It needs a licensed builder to coordinate the full scope of works. Behind every kitchen are structural adjustments, plumbing, electrical, tiling, plastering, and precise finishing that all need to come together in sequence. You brief us once, and we take care of the rest.

### Bathroom Renovations
Getting a bathroom right means getting the sequencing right. Waterproofing before tiling. Plumbing before fixtures. Every trade in the right order, held to the right standard. Nicon Built manages the whole thing on every bathroom job we take on.

### Carpentry and Joinery
Decking, pergolas, doors and frames, custom cabinetry, skirting boards. The kind of carpentry work that's built to last and finishes the job properly. In Melbourne's older homes, we match period joinery profiles so the new work sits naturally alongside the original.

### Painting and Plastering
A clean finish depends on what's underneath the paint. We repair the plaster first, let it cure, then paint. Interior and exterior, period homes and newer builds, single rooms and full houses.

### Rendering and Solid Plastering
External acrylic render and solid plaster finishing across Melbourne's inner south. Substrate preparation, heritage-appropriate repairs, and a clean surface ready for paint. Done properly before the next trade steps in.

### Tiling and Caulking
Kitchens, bathrooms, laundries, outdoor areas. Every tile laid consistently, every wet area sealed properly, waterproofed where required. Tiling and caulking go together — the difference between good work and average is obvious to anyone, even if they can't say exactly why.

### Plumbing
New installations, hot water systems, pipe repairs, and plumbing coordination within renovation projects. Carried out by licensed plumbers, coordinated by Nicon Built.

### Electrical
Lighting, power points, switchboard upgrades, and outdoor electrical. Carried out by licensed electricians, coordinated by Nicon Built.

### Property Maintenance
Whatever needs doing around the home, we coordinate every trade so you don't have to. Scheduling, sequencing, quality. One call, one team, done properly. Including emergency make-safe and insurance claim work when urgent response is needed.

### Pest Control
Termites, rodents, and general pests in Melbourne's older homes need prompt, licensed attention. Carried out by licensed pest technicians, coordinated by Nicon Built.

End-of-job cleans after renovations, bond cleans between tenancies, and regular scheduled cleaning. The build isn't finished until the home is ready to live in.

### Roofing
Leaks, damaged tiles, worn flashings, and gutter issues need prompt attention before they cause damage inside. Repairs across tile, metal, and period-style roofing, including heritage homes.

### Brickwork and Block Laying
New brick and block walls, repointing, chimney repairs and heritage brick matching across Melbourne's inner south. Period-accurate materials sourced through a specialist supplier network built across 30 years of heritage work.

### Concreting and Paving
Driveways, paths, exposed aggregate, polished concrete and paver installations. Outdoor hardscaping done with the right falls, finishes and drainage, coordinated as part of renovations or as standalone work.

### Heritage Renovations and Restorations
Heritage restoration is at the heart of what we do. We specialise in Victorian, Edwardian, and Federation homes, combining over 30 years of experience with a deep understanding of traditional building methods and materials. Our goal is to preserve the integrity of your home while delivering a result that stands the test of time.

### Heritage Home Extensions
Extending a heritage home in Melbourne's inner south is not the same as a standard extension. It requires a careful balance between new and old, ensuring the original building's character is respected while meeting strict heritage overlay requirements. With decades of experience in heritage construction, we understand what's required to achieve additions that feel natural, compliant, and true to the original architecture.
