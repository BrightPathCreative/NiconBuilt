## Headline
Concreting and Paving Melbourne

Driveways, paths, polished concrete and outdoor paving. Laid properly and built to last.

## Page copy
Concrete and paving work fails when the preparation is rushed. Compaction, falls for drainage, expansion joints, and the right finish for how the surface will actually be used. A driveway that holds water, or pavers that lift after the first wet season, are expensive to fix.

We handle concreting and paving across Melbourne's inner south. New driveways, paths and courtyards, exposed aggregate and polished concrete finishes, bluestone and paver installations, and repairs to existing hardscaping. Work that's done properly the first time.

On renovation and extension projects, outdoor hardscaping often comes last Ś but it still needs to be sequenced correctly with drainage, landscaping and any structural work underneath. Nicon Built coordinates every stage so the finished surface ties in cleanly with the rest of the build.

You brief us once. We manage scheduling, access, quality checks and handover. One team, one point of contact, no separate calls to individual contractors.

## FAQs

**Do you handle both concrete and paver installations?** Yes. Poured concrete driveways and paths, exposed aggregate finishes, and paver or bluestone installations are all within scope. We assess the site and recommend the right approach for how the area will be used.

**Can you repair cracked or damaged concrete?** Yes. Section replacements, resurfacing and full driveway replacements are common jobs. We assess the substrate and drainage first so the repair lasts.

**Is concreting part of a larger renovation project?** Often. New extensions, pool areas and outdoor entertaining spaces frequently include concreting and paving as part of the overall build. Nicon Built sequences this work with structural, landscaping and finishing trades.

**Do you install polished concrete floors indoors?** Yes. Polished concrete is a popular finish in modern renovations and new builds. Nicon Built coordinates the pour, curing and polishing as part of the broader project scope.
