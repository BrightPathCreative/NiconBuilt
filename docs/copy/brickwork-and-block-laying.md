## Headline
Brickwork and Block Laying Melbourne

New walls, repairs and repointing. Heritage brickwork matched properly.

## Page copy
Good brickwork is structural and visual at the same time. Straight courses, consistent mortar joints, clean corners, and bricks that match what's already on the home. Get any of that wrong and it shows immediately Ś especially on a period facade where the original brickwork sets the standard.

We handle brick and block laying across Melbourne's inner south. New garden walls, extension piers, chimney repairs, facade sections, and full repointing where mortar has deteriorated. Block work for garages, retaining walls and new-build structures.

In heritage homes, matching matters. Clinker bricks, lime mortars, tuck-pointing and period profiles need specialist knowledge and the right materials. Nicon Built sources period-matched brickwork through a supplier network built across 30 years of heritage work in Melbourne.

You brief us once. We coordinate licensed bricklayers, sequence their work with rendering, concreting and structural trades, and hold every stage to the same standard. No chasing individual tradies.

## What's included

- New brick and block walls, piers and garden walls

- Mortar repointing and brick replacement
- Period-matched heritage brickwork repairs

## FAQs

**Do you handle new brick walls as well as repairs?** Yes. New garden walls, extension brickwork, garage block walls and structural piers are all within scope. Repairs and new work are managed to the same standard.

**Can you match existing heritage brickwork?** Yes. Through a specialist supplier network, Nicon Built sources period-matched bricks and lime mortars that complement original construction on Melbourne's Victorian, Edwardian and Federation homes.

**Do brick repairs require a building permit?** Some structural brickwork does. As a VBA licensed builder, Nicon Built can assess each job and manage permit-required work that a handyman isn't qualified to undertake.

**Can brickwork be part of a larger renovation?** Absolutely. Brickwork is often sequenced with rendering, concreting, carpentry and structural trades on extensions and home renovations. Nicon Built coordinates the full scope.

**What areas do you cover?** Port Melbourne, Albert Park, Middle Park, South Melbourne, Elwood, Brighton, Hampton, Sandringham, Armadale, Malvern, South Yarra, Williamstown, Moorabbin, Bentleigh, Caulfield, Elsternwick, Camberwell, Hawthorn, Kew, and surrounding suburbs.
