## Page copy
Roof problems rarely stay on the roof. A leak in the valley iron shows up as a stain on the ceiling. Damaged tiles let water into the cavity where it sits unnoticed until the plaster starts to bubble. Worn flashings around a heritage chimney fail slowly, then all at once during a heavy downpour.

We handle roofing repairs and maintenance across Melbourne's inner south and bayside suburbs. Leak investigation, tile and metal roof repairs, flashing replacement, gutter coordination, and period-style roofing for heritage homes — all managed through Nick's trusted network of qualified roofers built over 30 years.

You brief us on the problem. Nick coordinates access, scopes the repair properly, and makes sure the work is done before it causes damage inside the home. In Melbourne's period housing stock, getting the roofing materials and profiles right matters as much as fixing the leak. We source period-accurate tiles and flashings where heritage homes require them.

Roofing is often part of a broader maintenance scope — gutter replacement, exterior painting, pest inspections in the roof cavity, or general property maintenance where multiple trades need to be sequenced in the right order. One call, one team, everything managed properly.
