## Headline
About Nicon Built | Melbourne Heritage and Renovation Builder

For over three decades, we have shaped the residential landscape of Melbourne's inner south. Here is our story, our process, and our commitment to building excellence.

## Built on Experience. Driven by Quality.
Founded in Port Melbourne, Nicon Built was established after decades of experience delivering some of Melbourne's largest and most recognised construction projects, including the Bolte Bridge, Burnley Tunnel, State Library redevelopment, and Melbourne Museum.

Working on projects of this scale built a strong foundation in precision, planning, and quality workmanship. These are the principles that continue to guide every project we deliver today.

## How We Work
With more than 30 years of industry experience, our approach is simple: understand your goals, communicate clearly, and deliver quality workmanship from start to finish.

Whether you're planning a renovation, extension, maintenance works, or a new build, we coordinate every trade required to complete the project efficiently and to the highest standard.

Already working with an architect or designer? We're happy to collaborate. If not, we can recommend trusted professionals we've worked with over many years.

From your initial consultation through to project completion, you'll deal directly with the team managing your project. We believe clear communication, regular updates, and hands-on project management are the key to achieving outstanding results.

## A Business Built on Relationships
Most of our work comes from referrals and repeat clients, a reflection of the trust we've built through quality workmanship and reliable service. Homeowners, architects, property managers, and commercial clients continue to recommend Nicon Built because we deliver on our commitments.

As a family-owned and operated business, we're committed to providing personalised service, attention to detail, and lasting relationships with every client. Every project is completed with the same focus on craftsmanship, professionalism, and care, regardless of its size or complexity.

## Our Process
Every project starts with a conversation. Contact us by phone or through our enquiry form, and we'll respond promptly to discuss your requirements.

For larger, more complex, or heritage projects, we'll visit your site to assess the work firsthand before providing a detailed, transparent estimate. By understanding the project from the outset, we can prepare an accurate estimate and minimise unexpected costs later in the process.

Once work begins, you'll be kept informed every step of the way. We believe in clear, proactive communication, so if any changes arise to the scope, timeline, or budget, you'll know before work proceeds, not after.

With over 30 years of industry experience, we're well equipped to manage the unexpected. Whether it's uncovering hidden structural issues, sourcing specialist materials, or navigating heritage requirements, we have the knowledge and experience to find practical solutions while keeping your project on track.

From initial consultation to final handover, our focus is on delivering quality workmanship, honest advice, and a finished result you'll be proud of.

## Who We Work With
We work with homeowners, architects, designers, and property professionals looking for a builder they can rely on to deliver quality workmanship and a well-managed project.

Many of our clients come to us once their plans, drawings, and approvals are in place and they're ready to build. They want an experienced team that will execute the project with care, communicate clearly, and deliver the finished result as intended.

We also work closely with architects and designers who trust us to bring their vision to life. We respect the design, follow the plans, and collaborate throughout the construction process to ensure every detail is delivered to a high standard.

Whether you're a homeowner planning a renovation or extension, or a design professional looking for a dependable building partner, we're committed to delivering projects with professionalism, craftsmanship, and attention to detail.
