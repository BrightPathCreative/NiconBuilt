## FAQs

**Do I need my plans before I contact you?** No. If your plans are done, we can get into quoting straight away. If you're still working through them, Nick is happy to talk through the scope early and can recommend architects he's worked with across Melbourne's inner south.

**What types of extensions do you take on?** All types. Single-storey rear extensions, first-floor additions, dual-occupancy additions, and full whole-home extensions. We also handle structural alterations such as wall removals and open-plan conversions where a building permit is required.

**How do you manage all the different trades?** Nick coordinates every trade himself. Tilers, plumbers, plasterers, electricians, painters. All from a trusted network built over 30 years. You don't need to find them, brief them, or chase them. One contact and Nick handles the whole job.

**What happens if something unexpected comes up mid-job?** Nick tells you straight away. Any change to scope, timeline, or cost gets communicated before any work proceeds. We don't sign off on variations without your knowledge. You hear about it first, not after.

**How long does a full home extension take?** Full home extensions typically range from three to eight months. Nick gives you a realistic timeline at the quoting stage based on your specific project and current trade availability.
